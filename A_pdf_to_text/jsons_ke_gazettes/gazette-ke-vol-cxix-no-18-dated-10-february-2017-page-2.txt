MINISTRY OF TOURISM 
ESTABLISHMENT OF THE USHANGA KENYA STEERING COMMITTEE AND SECRETARIAT FOR THE 
IMPLEMENTATION OF THE USHANGA KENYA INITIATIVE IT IS notified for the information of the general public that the Cabinet Secretary for Tourism has appointed the Ushanga Kenya Steering Committee and Secretariat to spearhead the implementation of the Ushanga Kenya Initiative. 
1. The Ushanga Kenya Steering Committee shall be the apex governance body for the implementation of the Initiative. 
2. The Committee shall comprise of the following— 
The Principal Secretary, Tourism—(Chairperson); 
The Principal Secretary, Culture; 
The Principal Secretary, Co-operatives; 
The Principal Secretary, Gender Affairs; 
The Principal Administrative Secretary, Office of the Deputy 
President; 
Two County Secretaries nominated by the Council of Governors from the target counties; 
The Chief Executive Officer, Council of Governors; 
The Co-ordinator—(Secretary); 
3. The Terms of Reference of the Committee are to— 
(a) provide policy guidance and oversight to the Ushanga Kenya Initiative; 
(b) co-ordinate actions required by the government at both levels to resolve matters brought to the steering committee for review and give final endorsements to the Ushanga Kenya Initiative annual work plans; 
(c) review and approve final budgets; 
(d) approve annual and any other progress reports; 
(e) approve all Ushanga Initiative expenditures in line with the work plans; 
(f) commission the preparation of all major reports including annual reports, audit reports, mid-term and end-term evaluation reports or any other reports that may inform the better execution of its mandate;
