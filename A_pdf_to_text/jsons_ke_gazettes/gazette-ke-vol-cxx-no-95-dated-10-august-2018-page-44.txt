Social aspect • Regular documentation of responses to questions and grievances submitted by the community adjacent to the project. 
• Creation of awareness on what and how the landfill operates. 
• Avail a copy of ESIA report at the land fill offices access to the local community who might have concerns with the operation of the landfill. 
• Develop and implement stakeholder engagement plan. 
• Apply the world bank grievance mechanism in conflict management. • Review the number of locally sourced materials including workforce to protect the proponent’s social license to operate. 
• Develop a continuous awareness campaign and training plan for the workers and neighbors (stakeholders). Population/demographic • Prioritizes job opportunities for the movement and local community first then employment compensate the deficit with the influx population. 
Public and workers health • Developing standard operating and safety procedures for landfill management. • Enclosing waste on transit. 
• Implement all necessary measures to ensure health and safety of workers and the general public during operation of the project as stipulated in OSHA 2007. 
• Ensuring strict access to site to authorized personnel only. 
• Compacting and applying cover on waste daily to minimize odor and prevent attraction of vermins and insects. 
• Develop pest management plan.

INTENDED DESTRUCTION OF COURT RECORDS 
IN ACCORDANCE with the Records Disposal (Courts) Rules, 10th August, 2018 notice is given that three (3) months after the date of publication of this notice, I intend to apply to the Chief Justice, for leave to destroy the records, books and papers of the Senior Principal Magistrate’s Court at Baricho as set out below: 
Criminal cases 1986–2015 
Traffic cases 1989–2016 
Miscellenous application 1998–2016 
A comprehensive list of all condemned records that qualify to be disposed under the Act can be obtained and perused at the Senior Principal Magistrate’s Court Registry, Baricho. 
Any person desiring the return of any exhibit in any of the above cases must make his/her claim within the time stipulated in this publication and should do so before the expiry of the notice. 
All exhibits to which no claim is substantiated before the destruction of the records shall under section 4 be deemed to be part of the records for the purposes of destruction. 
Dated the 17th July, 2018.
