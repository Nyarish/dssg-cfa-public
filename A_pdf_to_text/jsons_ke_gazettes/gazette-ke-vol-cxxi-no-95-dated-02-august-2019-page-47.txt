Impacts Proposed Mitigation Measures 
• Display communicative posters within site on HIV/AIDS related messages. 
• Employ security personnel to protect contractor assets. 
• Ensure the site has adequate lighting and signage. 
• Keep records of entry and exits for people and vehicles and materials to site. 
• Issue workers with uniforms or name tags and strictly enforce their use. 
Increased demand • The contractor to ensure prudential use of for water water resources. 
• Sensitize construction workers on the importance of proper water management. 
• Replace or repair leaking pipes supplying water. 
• Reuse of waste water. 
• Direct construction water runoff to areas where it can soak into the ground or be reused.
