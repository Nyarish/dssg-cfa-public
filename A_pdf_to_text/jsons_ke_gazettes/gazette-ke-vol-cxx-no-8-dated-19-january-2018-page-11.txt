THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF NYERI 
SPECIAL SITTING OF THE COUNTY ASSEMBLY OF NYERI 
NOTICE is given to all Honourable Members of the County Assembly of Nyeri that pursuant to Standing Order 27 (3) of the County Assembly of Nyeri Standing Orders, on the request of the Majority Leader and the Minority Leader, I have appointed Monday, 22nd January, 2018 as a day for a Special Sitting of the County Assembly of Nyeri. The Special Sitting shall be held in the County Assembly Chamber at 9.30 a.m. 
The business to be transacted at the Special Sitting shall be the tabling of the Nyeri County Integrated Development Plan 2018-2022. Dated the 17th day of January, 2018. 
JOHN KAGUCHIA,
