THE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONES ACT 
(No. 16 of 2015) 
SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONES REGULATIONS 
(L.N. 147 of 2016) 
SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONES APPLICATION AND LICENSING FEES 
IN EXERCISE of the powers conferred by section 39 (1) (i) of the Special Economic Zones Act and section 51 (1) (part X) of the Special Economic Zones (SEZ) Regulation, 2016, the Cabinet Secretary for Industry, Trade and Co-operatives, on recommendation of the Special Economic Zones Authority declares the following fees as the applicable fees for the following SEZ transactions as specified in the following schedule: 
SCHEDULE 
(a) Special Economic Zones application processing fee for designation and declaration shall be US$ 150. 
(b) Special Economic Zones Developers Licence fee shall be US$ 5,000 per annum. 
(c) Special Economic Zones Operators Licence fee shall be US$ 5,000 per annum. 
(d) Special Economic Zones Enterprise Licence fee shall be US$ 1,000 per annum. 
Dated the 11th December, 2017. 
ADAN MOHAMED, Cabinet Secretary for Industry, Trade and Co-operatives. 19th January, 2018
